The World

Drovers' Cross — a warm solarpunk-Western boomtown.

Old-West craft meets a hand-built, sunlit future — airships on the horizon, copper solar sails on the rooftops, ridge windmills — and the whole gang's welcome. The cast stays pure Western; the blend lives in the world.

The Herd is the outfit you join; the Exchange is Flipcash itself — where you join the herd. (Its in-world art is coming.)
